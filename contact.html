Frequently Asked Questions

How does TravelEase personalize recommendations?

We use an algorithm that considers your preferences, past travels, budget, and travel style to suggest destinations that match your interests.

Is TravelEase free to use?

Yes, our basic recommendation service is completely free. We offer premium features for advanced planning tools.

How quickly can I expect a response to my inquiry?

We typically respond within 24 hours on business days. For urgent matters, please call our support line.

Can I book flights and hotels through TravelEase?

Currently, we focus on recommendations and planning. We partner with trusted booking platforms for actual reservations.
